We believe that the youths have rights and their voices should be heard.
Young women are our priority and we lead them to persons that fight
for their rights in the society.

Advocacy and Lobbying

Through capacity building, we champion for laws and policies that accommodate women's views and are co-opted on decision-making. We educate the youths and girls to know and make use of their rights. We do mobilisation at the grassroots through communiy outreaches to raise awareness. We collaborate and network with other organizatios like the Human Rights Organizations to amplify the voices of women and youths.
